About Us

Our mission is to empower young students to be the Inventors and Creators.


At Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore), the keen intellect of students has been honed and sharpened by the skilled hands of expert faculty. Also, the stimulating environs of a new IIT have created opportunities for students’ leadership skills and innovative thinking capabilities to spread their wings in full flight, unrestrained by orthodoxy.
